Description

Of course, your clients are curious to know who will be in their group! Let's show them their fellow students.

First, introduce some changes to the course_details template. Modify your main page template to show the list of students using filtering on the connected fields of two models in the database.

If it works properly, congratulations! You have a skeleton for your online school's site. As it's been indicated earlier, you can develop it further and turn it into an effective instrument for business.

Objective
  • Change the course_details template and display a list of students.
